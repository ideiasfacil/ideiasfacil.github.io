Airport Security 3D

Airport Security 3D is a simulation game where you take on the role of an airport security officer. Using 3D cartoon models, your mission is to ensure safety by arresting criminals with fake IDs and hidden weapons, while approving innocent passengers. At the counter, carefully verify passenger names against their ID cards, and at the security checkpoint, inspect their baggage for contraband.
