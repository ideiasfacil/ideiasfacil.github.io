Overview

Papas Cupcakeria is a fun and engaging restaurant management game where players take on the role of a cupcake chef. Your goal is to prepare and serve delicious cupcakes to a variety of customers, customizing each order with different batters, fillings, frostings, and toppings. As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new recipes and ingredients, allowing for even more creative combinations. Manage your time wisely to keep customers happy and earn tips, all while creating the ultimate cupcake experience!
